Got questions?

Frequently asked questions.

Do you offer 24/7 emergency plumbing?

Yes. Damien and the on-call crew take turns covering the phone, day and night, so you always reach a real person — never a call centre — and the crew typically reaches most of Cape Town within 45 minutes for genuine emergencies.

Do you give a quote before starting work?

Always. You'll get an upfront price after we've diagnosed the problem on site, and nothing starts until you approve it — no surprise invoices.

Do you handle anything beyond plumbing?

Yes — alongside plumbing, geysers and solar, the crew also handles blocked drains, general maintenance, roofing, painting and full bathroom or kitchen renovations.

What areas of Cape Town do you cover?

Sea Point, Camps Bay, Green Point, Claremont, Constantia, Century City, Bellville, Table View, Muizenberg, Durbanville and the surrounding suburbs. Not sure if you're covered? Just call.

Is Apex Plumbing licensed and insured?

Yes — fully licensed and insured, with over 10 years on the tools. Every completed job is backed by a 12-month workmanship guarantee.
